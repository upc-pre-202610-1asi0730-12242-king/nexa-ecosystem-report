# Branching and Commits

Commit quality is essential for team coordination and academic auditing. All commits must follow the **Conventional Commits 1.0.0** specification.

## Commit Message Template

```text
<type>(<scope>): <short action summary>

Context:
- What area, milestone or branch this commit belongs to.

Changes:
- What was changed.
- What was added, corrected or normalized.

Reason:
- Why the change was necessary.

Validation:
- What was reviewed or checked before committing.
```

## Allowed Types & Scopes
Please refer to the [.github/CONTRIBUTING.md](../.github/CONTRIBUTING.md) guidelines for the complete lists of types and scopes.
