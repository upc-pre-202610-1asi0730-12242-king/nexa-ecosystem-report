# Product Scope

This page outlines the target customer segments, user goals, and Lean UX assumptions defining the product scope of the Nexa ecosystem.

## Target Segments

1. **Warehouse Operators**:
   - *Goal*: Streamline picking, packing, and loading operations while maintaining cold storage standards.
   - *Pain Points*: Cluttered layouts, missing temperature threshold logs.
2. **Logistics Dispatchers**:
   - *Goal*: Coordinate shipment dispatches, optimize delivery routing, and monitor active transit alerts.
   - *Pain Points*: Fragmented communication, lack of real-time GPS tracking.
3. **B2B Buyers**:
   - *Goal*: Place purchase orders, view live stock catalogs, and track delivery status.
   - *Pain Points*: Delayed dispatch notifications, opaque stock levels.

## Lean UX Assumptions & Hypotheses

- **UX Hypothesis 1**: By providing real-time temperature warning banners to warehouse coordinators, we can reduce product spoilage due to thermal shock by 30%.
- **UX Hypothesis 2**: Implementing a fluid, mobile-responsive buyer catalog table will increase order frequency and customer retention.
- **UX Hypothesis 3**: Providing dispatchers with an auto-fit quick-action grid will reduce administrative dispatch setup times.
